Classic roguelike RPG

Depths

Pick a class — Warrior, Ranger, Alchemist — and dive for the Amulet on depth 10. Abilities, level-up boons, cursed gear, shops, champions, and a Soul Shrine that upgrades every future run.

Click a tile to walk · click monsters to fight · click abilities
 🌀
Survivors / auto-shooter

Swarm

Your hero chases the cursor, weapons fire on their own — now with sound, evolutions, artillery that shells campers, elite walls, and double bosses. Surviving 30:00 must be earned.

Move cursor to steer · dodge the red circles · click upgrades
 ♻️
Idle tactics

Loopline

Your hero walks the loop alone — you shape the world. Adjacency synergies (Blood Grove, River, corrupted Villages), gear rarities, a fair Colossus at loop 4, a two-phase Lich at 8, NG+ and a camp meta-shop.

Click a card, click a tile · click gear to equip
 ⚔️
Strategic auto-battler

Warband

Pick a commander, draft around four faction synergies, equip spoils on your units, and scout the exact enemy lineup before every fight. Economy with interest — every gold is a decision.

Click a unit, click a slot · train, freeze, reroll, equip
 🂠
Card dungeon

Dungeon Deal

A 4×4 grid of fate — you move into every card you take. Keys, spells with targeting, relics, level-ups, events, a mini-boss on floor 5 and a two-stage Depth Lord on floor 10.

Click adjacent cards · hover to preview exact outcomes
